Submarine Building Course Program

Week 1:Introduction to Submarine Design

In the first week,we will cover the basics of submarine design,including the history of submarines,keyprinciples,and the
different types of submarines.Students will gain a solid foudation in submarine construction and operation.

Week 2:Materials and Construction

This week,we'll dive into the materials used in submarine construction and the techniques involved in building a submarine.
Toppics will include pressure hulls,ballast systems,and various engineering considerations for submersible vessels.

week 3:Propulsion Systems

Week 3 will focus on propulsion systems for systems for submarines.Students will learn about different type of propulsion,such as
nuclear,diesel-electric,and air-indipendent propulsion.We'll also discuss the pros and cons of each system.

Week 4:Submarine operations and Safety

During this week,we'll explora various submarine operation and safety measure.Topics include underwater navigation,
communication,life support systems,and emergency procedures.Student will gain insights into the challenges of operating a
submarine and ensuring the well-being of the crew during extended underwater mission.

Final Project: Submarine Design and Presentation

During the final week,student will work on a group project to design a hypothetical submarine for a specific mission. Each
group will present their design to the class,explaning the rationale behind their choices.
