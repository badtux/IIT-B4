Submarine Building Course Program

Week 1: Introduction to
Submarine design

In the first week, We will cover the basics of subamrine
design,including the history of submarines,key
principles,and the different types of submarines.
students will gain a solid foundation in submarine
construction and operation.

Week 2:Materials and
Construction

This week , we'll dive into the materials used in
submarine construction and the techniques involved in
building a submarine. Topics will include pressure hulls.
ballast systems, and various engineering considerations
for submersible vessels.

Week:3 propulsions systems

Week 3 will focus on propulsion systems
submarines. students will learn about diferent types of
propulsion, such as nuclear, diesel-electric, and air-
independent propulsion. we'll also discuss the pros and
cons of each system.

Week 4;:Submarine Operations
and Safety

During this week, we'll explore various submarine
operations and safety measures. Topics include
underwater navigation, communication, life support
systems, and emergency procedures.students will gain
insights into the challenges of operating a submarine
and ensuring the well-being of the crew during extended
underwater missions.

Final Project: Submarine
Design and Presentation

During the final week,students will work on a group
project to design a hypothetical submarine for a specific
mission.Each group will present their design to the
class, explaining the rationale behind the choices.

Prerequisties

optional prerequisties for students who want to further
enhance their understanding:

  1. A basic understanding of engineering principles.
  2. Familiarity with physics and mathematics
  3. An interest in marine technology and exploration

Course Duration

The course will run for a total of 4 weeks , with one 2-
hour session per week.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course will
receive a certifiacte of completion in submarine
building.
